Google Photos Backup to save space on iCloud

Nobody wants to lose their photos or personal files.

The purpose of this article is to help you save on iCloud storage usage by using Google Photos Backup for your photos and videos and iCloud for the rest of your iPhone, iPod or iPad.

Costs

iCloud 50GB - $1.49 AU per month

Google Photos Backup - Unlimited option is free.

Method

Set iCloud to backup your iPhone except photos and videos.

Install Google Photos Backup

In Google Photos Backup, Turn on backup over Wi-Fi in Google Photos Backup.
