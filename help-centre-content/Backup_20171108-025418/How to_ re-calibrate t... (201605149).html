How to: re-calibrate the home button on the iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch

The home button may become less sensitive making it difficult to go back to the home screen. Re-calibrating your home button this way will work on your iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch.

  1. Launch one of the stock apps on your iDevice (Weather, iTunes, Stocks, etc.)
  2. Press and hold the power button until you see the “slide to power off” prompt, then release the power button.
  3. Press and hold you home button until the the “slide to power off” prompt disappears and the app force quits.

You should now be on your home screen and your home button should be re-calibrated.
