Will I keep my address book?

Yes, we can transfer your address book to Google Contacts.

Sources include Outlook Express, Microsoft Outlook, Windows Live Mail, Thunderbird and most webmail services. 

Are there any exceptions?
Autocomplete list is not transferred.Each time that you send a new message in Outlook, the emails that you type in To/Cc fields, are automatically inserted into the AutoComplete list, so in the next time that you type an email address, Outlook automatically completes the right email address for you. 

But I really want my autocomplete list transferred.
